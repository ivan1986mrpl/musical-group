Band “Music band”

True rock that rumbles in your heart.

“Music band” is a mix of powerful guitar riffs, honest lyrics, and explosive stage energy. We play for those who value live sound, freedom, and the power of music.

Our concerts are always about connection with the crowd, drive, and emotion. Join us at upcoming shows and feel the sound live!

Our Story

The band "Music band" was born from a passion for music that’s felt with the heart. We began our journey in a small studio in the center of Kyiv, where every chord and word was filled with emotion. That’s where our first songs were born, and we learned to play as one.

Over the years, we’ve performed on dozens of stages — from small clubs to large festivals. Our music is a unique blend of traditional rock and modern elements that make it recognizable. We’re not afraid to experiment with sound, adding electronic vibes and folk instruments.

Each of our shows is not just a concert but a true event. We share our energy with the audience and create a one-of-a-kind atmosphere. We believe music has the power to unite people, build communities, and inspire change.

Our group consists of experienced musicians, each bringing a unique style and vision. Maksym creates incredible guitar riffs, Olena captivates with her vocals, and Taras keeps the rhythm that makes hearts beat in sync with the music.
